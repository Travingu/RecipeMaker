  • Recipe 1: Sample dish with ingredients Serves 4 | Prep: 15 min | Cook: 30 min

    Ingredients

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1/2 cup butter, softened
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract

    Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Mix flour and sugar in a large bowl.
    3. Add butter, eggs, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
    4. Pour into a greased baking pan.
    5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  • Recipe 2: Another delicious option Serves 2 | Prep: 10 min | Cook: 20 min

    Ingredients

    • 1 lb chicken breast
    • 1 cup vegetables
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions

    1. Season chicken with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
    2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
    3. Cook chicken for 6-7 minutes per side.
    4. Add vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
    5. Serve hot and enjoy!
  • Recipe 3: Quick and easy meal Serves 6 | Prep: 5 min | Cook: 25 min

    Ingredients

    • 3 cups rice
    • 6 cups water
    • 1 can diced tomatoes
    • 1 onion, diced
    • 1 tsp salt

    Instructions

    1. Rinse rice thoroughly.
    2. Combine rice, water, and salt in a pot.
    3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
    4. Simmer for 20 minutes until water is absorbed.
    5. Fluff with a fork and serve.
